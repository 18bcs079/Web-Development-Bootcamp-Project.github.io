CHHOTA BHEEM

Bheem is an adventurous and fun-loving nine-year-old who is gifted with extraordinary strength.
This power is a boon for Dholakpur, Bheem's village,which is constantly under threat.
This little kid has a BIG heart and he always uses his strength to help those who are wronged or needy.
Bheem loves food and has a special weakness for laddoos, which in fact, give him a surge of energy and make him even stronger.
